ğŸ“Š Key Insights & Findings

Approximately 20.5% of transactions are classified as high risk
Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and Bank Transfers contribute almost equally (~33% each) to total transactions
Specific banks and branches consistently show higher suspicious activity, indicating potential risk concentration
Monthly transaction trends remain stable for most of the year, with a significant decline in November
A small segment of customers accounts for a large share of total transaction value, highlighting concentration risk
A credit-to-debit ratio of ~0.99 indicates balanced inflow and outflow across accounts
